Porkolt (Hungarian Stew)

Description:

A flavorful stew, Pörkölt is redolent with the fragrance of paprika and bell peppers.

Ingredients:

  • 5 slices bacon, diced
  • 2 large onions, diced
  • 5 pounds boneless pork chops
  • 2 (14 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
  • 1 large yellow bell pepper, seeded and diced
  • 2 cups reduced-fat sour cream

Steps:

  1. Place the bacon in a large, deep skillet, and cook over medium-high heat until evenly browned, about 10 minutes
  2. Heat a small amount of the reserved bacon drippings in the skillet again over medium-high heat
  3. Heat a small amount of the bacon drippings in the skillet; cook and stir the bell pepper in the hot drippings until softened and fragrant
  4. Pour the tomatoes with liquid and beef broth into a stockpot and place the pot over medium-high heat
  5. Bring a pot with lightly-salted water and bring to a rolling boil; add the egg noodles to the water and return to a boil
  6. Ladle the stew over the drained noodles to serve.
